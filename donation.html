Donate Clothes

Join our movement valuing teamwork and community, gaining access to a unique collection while giving back through our clothing donation feature.

Our Cycle

Join the Trend Cycles movement by donating your clothing. Together, we can create vibrant fashion and build a thriving community for a better future!

Send with Care

Please ensure all donated clothing is clean, neatly packaged, and items you are comfortable parting with, as returns are not possible once donated .

Rewards For Donating

When you pay the $10 service fee, you are not only contributing towards a more sustainable environment but also receiving a $2 store reward. This reward will be added to your account once we have received your donated items.

Get Excited

Donating your old clothes embraces sustainability, support social causes, witness fashion innovation, and community.

Cleaning Time

We ensures that the garments are hygienic, fresh, and ready for their next life.

Fabric Chocies

Unneeded items are sent to a specialized recycling center where textiles are transformed into reusable components.

Design

We embrace collaborations and are always open to new fashion ideas, making you a potential partner for future endeavors.

Get a Label to Donate Items

Please fill out the form below to receive a downloadable shipping label for your items. Once you have printed out the label, prepare a suitable package that can be mailed through the United States Postal Service. Please note that by shipping out your items, you agree to our policies, and it's important to understand that once shipped, you will not be able to retrieve the items.
