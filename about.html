Who are We?

Our mission is to establish a fashion brand that not only stays at the forefront of the latest trends but also makes a positive impact on the environment. At TC, we celebrate diversity, promote inclusivity, and foster a strong sense of community. We firmly believe that our brand owes its existence to our incredible community.
Trend Cycles showcases diverse collections of patchwork clothing and accessories. All the items listed on the site are from donated or recycled fabrics and materials. Anyone can donate their unwated clothing to us. To do it you will need to fill out the form on our Donation Page

Behind the scenes

Actively collaborating with other businesses that sell recycled materials.At Trend Cycle, we are committed to minimizing waste. Any excess fabric and unused materials from our production process are carefully disposed of and sent for ethical and sustainable remanufacturing.

Our Designers

Trend Cycle is proudly located in the heart of NYC, at 132-138 Mulberry St. This vibrant space serves as our creative hub, where our talented designers collaborate to bring forth the garments for each upcoming season.Every design is meticulously hand crafted in-house, ensuring quality and attention to detail.

Connect with us

We are always excited to curate new items and collaborate with new designers. If you're a fashion designer interested in collaborating with us, please reach out to our team and Email Us. We carefully review all collaboration requests and select designers each season to be feature on our website.
